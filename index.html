About Us

eDC | JIT is an entrepreneurial club dedicated to empowering and uplifting young and emerging entrepreneurs. At the heart of our mission is the goal of providing a nurturing platform for aspiring visionaries, guiding them towards their path of success while instilling them with the confidence to embrace the challenges of entrepreneurship. Our club takes pride in connecting with budding entrepreneurs, offering them a helping hand to step into the forefront of the business world. We believe that collaboration and networking are key ingredients for entrepreneurial growth, and thus, we actively engage with various entrepreneurs and business minds to foster meaningful connections. Together, we organize workshops at the college level, creating a dynamic learning environment where ideas are shared, knowledge is exchanged, and innovation thrives.

Events

The Startup Idea Pitching Session.

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell organized a startup pitching cell for its core team members. The event featured pitches from various domains, including education, healthcare, and technology. the pitches were well-received by the audience, and the event was a great opportunity for the team members to learn about new startup ideas and to network with other entrepreneurship.


Events

EDC-JIT Inauguration.

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell JIT held and inauguration event for its new club. The event was a great success, with all the department's HOD's present. The chief guest was S HIRIYANNA, Retd. Group Head, GEOSAT, ISRO who gave a motivational speech to the students.


Events

The Auditions To form EDC-JIT.

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell Conducted auditions for its board members and core team members. The event was a great success, with over 75 members attending. The auditions were held in a competitive but friendly atmosphere, and the judges were impressed with the talent and enthusiasm of the participants.

Documentations

The eDC proudly hosted an awe-inspiring inauguration event on 3rd July to mark the beginning of an exciting new chapter. With immense enthusiasm and a touch of grandeur, this event celebrated the official launch of Entrepreneurship Development Club and served as a platform to showcase the organization's commitment to innovation, progress, and the pursuit of excellence. The inaugural event was organised in the main auditorium of Jyothy Institute of Technology campus.
