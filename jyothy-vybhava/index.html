About the Event

Embark on a journey of innovation and excellence at the Jyothy Institute of Technology's annual extravaganza - Jyothy Vaibhava! This grand event is a harmonious convergence of entrepreneurial spirits, technical prowess, inventive minds, and creative artistry, creating a vibrant stage that nurtures comprehensive student growth.

With a diverse array of events, Jyothy Vaibhava is meticulously crafted to inspire and cultivate talents across various domains. From showcasing cutting-edge technological projects to providing a vibrant canvas for artistic expression, this extravaganza offers a multidimensional experience. Students find themselves immersed in an atmosphere that not only encourages exploration but also celebrates experimentation.

Jyothy Vaibhava goes beyond being just an event; it becomes a cornerstone in the educational journey, shaping well-rounded individuals poised to tackle the challenges of the future. As students engage in different events, they not only revel in their achievements but also contribute significantly to the legacy of innovation and excellence at the Jyothy Institute of Technology. Join us in this celebration of talent, creativity, and the spirit of exploration, making Jyothy Vaibhava an eye-catching spectacle of educational brilliance!

Black Market is not just an event, it's an immersive experience where participants receive a mystery product. Teams traverse the campus, pitching their ideas, securing investments. The team with the highest currency reigns supreme, winning exciting cash prizes!

The twist? Our esteemed teachers will act as investors, armed with fake currency. It's a high-stakes challenge as teams pitch their product, working hard to secure investments from the teachers. This interaction requires strategic thinking, communication, and the ability to adapt on the fly.

As teams navigate through the campus, they'll encounter diverse environments, testing their ability to sell their product. The objective is clear: accumulate the highest amount of fake currency by the end of the journey. The intensity builds as teams vie for the coveted top spots, with the promise of substantial cash prizes.


Rules:

  • • Team of 2-4 members can be formed.
  • • The team with the highest amount of fake currency accumulated at the end of the event wins the event.

Get ready for an event that combines the excitement of a quiz and the strategy of a cricket auction! The IPL Auction is divided into two rounds, ensuring a dynamic and engaging experience for all participants.

Round 1: Elimination Quiz The journey begins with an elimination quiz where teams will test their cricket knowledge. The top 10 teams from the quiz will advance to the next round.

Round 2: Auction In the auction round, qualified teams will be given a digital currency of approximately 50 crore. On the screen, players will be displayed, each with a base price and a ranking. Teams must strategically use their digital currency to purchase players and build a team of 11 members. The catch? The players' rankings play a crucial role, and only teams with a complete line-up will be qualified for evaluation.

Rules:

  • • The first round consists of a quiz. The top 10 teams qualify for the next round.
  • • The second round is the auction round. A digital currency of amount 50cr will be provided to every team.
  • • The participants should build a dream team consisting of 11 players.
  • • The team with the highest ranked dream team at the end of the auction wins the event.
