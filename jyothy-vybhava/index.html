IPL Auction


Get ready for the ultimate cricketing spectacle – the IPL Auction Extravaganza! It's more than just bidding; it's a battle for cricketing supremacy and strategy.
Event overview:
Event name: IPL AUCTION
Theme: Strategy, money management
Description:
Get ready for an event that combines the excitement of a quiz and the strategy of a cricket auction! The IPL Auction is divided into two rounds, ensuring a dynamic and engaging experience for all participants.
Round 1: Elimination Quiz
The journey begins with an elimination quiz where teams will test their cricket knowledge. The top 10 teams from the quiz will advance to the next round.
Round 2: Auction
In the auction round, qualified teams will be given a digital currency of approximately 50 crore. On the screen, players will be displayed, each with a base price and a ranking. Teams must strategically use their digital currency to purchase players and build a team of 11 members. The catch? The players' rankings play a crucial role, and only teams with a complete line-up will be qualified for evaluation.
Prizes:
The total prize pool is a whooping ₹7500, making victory not just a triumph of cricket strategy but also a rewarding one.
1st Prize: ₹5000
2nd Prize: ₹2500
Event Details:
Date: 12th January (tentative)
Registration: Teams of 2-4 can register for an entry fee of ₹200 per team.
Venue: JIT Campus
Timings: 2:00 am to 3:30 pm
IPL Auction brings strategy, knowledge, and excitement together! Register your team, brush up on your cricket trivia, and get ready to strategize and bid in the auction. Build the dream team and compete for the top spot!
